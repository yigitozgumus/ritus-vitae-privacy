Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Ritus Vitae is designed with privacy at its core. Your habit tracking data stays on your device and, if you choose, syncs privately through your personal iCloud account. We don't collect, sell, or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Create

When you use Ritus Vitae, you create and manage the following information:

  • Habit Information: Habit names, colors, reasons for tracking, creation dates, and archive status
  • Completion Records: Dates and times when you mark habits as completed
  • App Settings: Your preferences for app appearance, color scheme, and feature configurations
  • Color Locks: Your saved color preferences for year views

1.2 Data We Don't Collect

Ritus Vitae does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Contacts or other device data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • Local Storage: All your habit data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Display and Visualization: Your data is used to show your habit progress, year views, and statistics
  • Export Functionality: You can export your data for backup or migration purposes
  • Import Functionality: You can import previously exported data

3. iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)

If you subscribe to Ritus Vitae Pro, you have the option to enable iCloud synchronization:

  • Your Private iCloud: Data syncs through your personal iCloud account, not our servers
  • End-to-End Privacy: We cannot access your iCloud data. Apple encrypts data in transit and at rest
  • User Control: You can disable iCloud sync at any time through device settings
  • Premium Requirement: iCloud sync is only available to users with an active subscription

For more information about Apple's iCloud privacy and security, visit: Apple's iCloud Security Overview

4. In-App Purchases

Ritus Vitae offers optional premium subscriptions and lifetime purchases:

  • Apple Handles Transactions: All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
  • No Payment Information: We never see or store your payment information
  • Subscription Verification: We only verify subscription status through Apple's StoreKit framework
  • No Third-Party Payment Processors: All transactions go through Apple exclusively

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SwiftData: Apple's modern data persistence framework
  • Device Encryption: Protected by your device's built-in encryption when you enable device passcode
  • App Sandboxing: Isolated from other apps per iOS security model

5.2 iCloud Storage (When Enabled)

When you enable iCloud sync:

  • Data is stored in your private iCloud container
  • Apple encrypts data both in transit and at rest
  • Data is subject to Apple's iCloud security protocols

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Your iCloud: When you enable sync, data moves to your personal iCloud account
  • Export Feature: When you explicitly export data, you control where it goes
  • Screenshot Sharing: When you use the screenshot feature, you control if and where to share the image

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your habit data directly within the app
  • Export: Export your complete data at any time in a portable format
  • Delete: Delete individual habits or all your data through the app
  • iCloud Control: Enable or disable iCloud sync through device settings
  • App Deletion: Deleting the app removes all local data (iCloud data persists in your iCloud account)

8. Children's Privacy

Ritus Vitae does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not have age restrictions. Since we don't collect personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates or release notes
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

10. International Users

Ritus Vitae is available worldwide through the App Store. Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account, subject to Apple's data residency policies for your region.

11. Data Retention

Your data is retained:

  • Locally: Until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • iCloud: According to your iCloud storage settings and Apple's retention policies
  • Deleted Habits: Archived habits can be permanently deleted from the app at any time

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide the app's functionality
  • Legitimate Interest: Operating and improving the app
  • Consent: For optional features like iCloud sync

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the CCPA. However, since Ritus Vitae does not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA, these provisions do not apply to our app's data practices.
